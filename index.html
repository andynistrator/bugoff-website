Our Story

At Bugoff, we believe everyone deserves effective protection from mosquitoes without harsh chemicals or unpleasant experiences. Our journey began with a simple question: why should effective mosquito protection come with downsides like strong chemical smells, skin irritation, or ineffective natural alternatives?

Founded by a team of researchers and outdoor enthusiasts in Malaysia, we set out to create a better solution. After extensive research and testing, we developed our unique formulas that combine the proven effectiveness of Picaridin with natural oils for enhanced protection and a pleasant experience.

Today, Bugoff offers a range of mosquito repellent products designed to meet different needs — from daily family protection to heavy-duty outdoor adventures — all without the downsides of traditional repellents.
