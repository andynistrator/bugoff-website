About Bugoff

Our Story

At Bugoff, we believe everyone deserves effective protection from mosquitoes without harsh chemicals or unpleasant experiences. Our journey began with a simple question: why should effective mosquito protection come with downsides like strong chemical smells, skin irritation, or ineffective natural alternatives?

Founded by a team of researchers and outdoor enthusiasts in Malaysia, we set out to create a better solution. After extensive research and testing, we developed our unique formulas that combine the proven effectiveness of Icaridin with natural oils for enhanced protection and a pleasant experience.

Today, Bugoff offers a range of mosquito repellent products designed to meet different needs - from daily family protection to heavy-duty outdoor adventures - all without the downsides of traditional repellents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bugoff safe for children and pets?
Yes, our water-based formula is designed to be safe for the whole family, including children when used as directed. It's DEET-free and enhanced with natural oils. For pets, while generally safe, we recommend consulting a veterinarian for direct application.
How long does Bugoff protection last?
Our Family & Outdoor Water-Based formula provides 8-12 hours of protection, while our Heavy-Duty Oil-Based Compound formula offers almost entire day of protection, depending on activity and environmental conditions.
What is Icaridin, and why do you use it?
Icaridin is a synthetic compound that mimics the natural repellent properties of a plant-derived chemical called piperine. It's recommended by organizations like the CDC and EPA as a highly effective and safe alternative to DEET, offering strong protection against a wide range of biting insects.
Can I use Bugoff on my clothing?
Yes, Bugoff can be safely applied to both exposed skin and clothing. Mosquitoes can bite through thin fabrics, so applying to clothing ensures comprehensive protection.
