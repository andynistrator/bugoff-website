                    <h3 class="text-xl font-bold mb-2">What is Picaridin and why is it better than DEET?</h3>

                    <p>Picaridin is a highly effective repellent ingredient that offers comparable protection to DEET but without the downsides. It's odorless, non-greasy, doesn't damage plastics or synthetic fabrics, and is less likely to cause skin irritation. Our formulas use 10-20% Picaridin for reliable protection.</p>

                </div>

                

                <!-- FAQ Item 2 -->

                <div class="mb-6">

                    <h3 class="text-xl font-bold mb-2">Which Bugoff product should I choose?</h3>

                    <p>If you need everyday protection for the family, choose our water-based formula with 10% Picaridin. For outdoor adventures, camping, or high-risk areas, choose our heavy-duty oil-based formula with 20% Picaridin for maximum protection.</p>

                </div>

                    <p>Our water-based formula is designed to be family-friendly and is safe for adults and children when used as directed. The heavy-duty formula is recommended for adults and children over 12 years due to its higher concentration of active ingredients.</p>
