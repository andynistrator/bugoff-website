How to Apply

Follow these simple steps for 8-12 hours of total protection.

1

Shake Well

Ensure the active compound is perfectly blended before every single use.

2

Fully Compress

Compress nozzle fully to deliver a fine, consistent mist for even coverage.

3

Spray Exposed Skin

Apply thoroughly to all exposed areas of the body, avoiding eyes and mouth.

4

Cover Thin Fabrics

Bugs can bite through thin fabrics so spray on clothing evenly for full defense.

5

Reapply When Needed

Maintain your shield by reapplying after heavy sweating or contact with water.

6

Keep in Cool Place

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to preserve the formula.
